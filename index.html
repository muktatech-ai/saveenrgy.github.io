Action Plan

TOP WAYS TO
CUT YOUR BILL

01 💡

Switch to LED Bulbs

LED bulbs use 75% less energy than traditional incandescents and last up to 25 times longer. Replace high-use fixtures first for the fastest payback.

Save ~$75/yr per home
02 🌡️

Smart Thermostat

A programmable or learning thermostat can cut heating and cooling costs by 10–12%. Set it to lower temps when you're asleep or away automatically.

Save ~$180/yr
03 🔌

Kill Phantom Loads

Standby power — TVs, chargers, microwaves — accounts for 5–10% of your bill. Use smart power strips to cut power completely when devices are not in use.

Save ~$100/yr
04 🚿

Low-Flow Showerheads

Heating water is 18% of your energy use. A low-flow showerhead paired with shorter showers can dramatically reduce your water-heating costs.

Save ~$70/yr
05 🌞

Strategic Window Shading

Thermal curtains and blinds block summer heat gain and winter heat loss. This passive approach can reduce HVAC load by up to 7% at zero recurring cost.

Save ~$60/yr
06 🧺

Cold Water Washing

90% of a washing machine's energy goes to heating water. Switching to cold cycles for most loads saves energy without sacrificing clean results.

Save ~$40/yr
